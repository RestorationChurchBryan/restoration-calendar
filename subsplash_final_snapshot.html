Surge

April 27, 2025 from 6:00 - 7:30pm

Surge is our Sunday evening environment that comes from Habakkuk 2:14, which talks about the earth being filled with the knowledge of the Lord as the waters cover the sea. We want the gospel to surge forward in our youth and community. Surge is a fun environment for students to connect, bring friends, and grow in their faith.
